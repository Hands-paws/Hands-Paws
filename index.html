Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients are used in the treats?

Our treats are made with all-natural, dog-friendly ingredients including Whole Wheat Flour, Peanut Butter, Canned Pumpkin, Ground Cinnamon, Eggs, Salt, and Water. No artificial preservatives, flavors, or colors!
